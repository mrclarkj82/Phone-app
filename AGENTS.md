# Codex Notes

- Before committing or pushing any new assignment type, preview the teacher assignment builder and check that every preview card has enough vertical spacing. Graphs, coordinate grids, tables, fractions, radicals, and answer keys must stay inside their cards without overlapping the next problem.
- For graph-based assignments, verify the preview list, student problem cards, and teacher View Work cards. Do not ship until the cards grow around the graph instead of letting the graph overflow.
